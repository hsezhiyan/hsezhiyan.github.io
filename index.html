    About Me

    My name is Hari, and I'm a recent graduate from UC Davis, with a Bachelors of Science in Computer Science and Engineering. Whilst at Davis, I worked with Prof. Premkumar Devanbu on Transformer models for automatic code patching and an empirical study of the effectiveness of BLEU on code-to-comment generation tasks. I also worked with Dr. Uma Srivatsa from the UC Davis Health Center on creating ML models to detect ventricular tachycardia in patients. I currently work as an ML engineer at Zillow Group in Seattle.
